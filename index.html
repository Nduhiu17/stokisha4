Frequently Asked Questions

How can I access stokisha?

Stokisha App is available on the Google Playstore and via USSD by dialing *483*67#

What do I require to apply for a Stokisha loan?

Kenyan National Identity Card document,Business Registration Certificate and or License 6-months Mpesa transaction.

What is the loan qualification createria for Stokisha?

We look at:

  • Your Mpesa statement activity
  • Your curent business needs
  • Whether you have been listed on the CRB(Credit Reference Bureau)
  • And our internal credit scoring criteria
  • Age of your business (not less than 6 months)

Why does Stokisha need my ID?

To enable us to authenticate your indentity, phone number against Safaricom details and MPESA statement. Furthermore, we will also use it to run CRB check.

How long will my application process take to get an approval or a decline?

Not more than 30 Minutes

Why was my loan rejected?

Because you may have not met our minimal loan approval criteria.

Who can apply for a loan?

Business that are looking to get financing for ther stock both formal and informal.

How do I apply for a loan?

If you have an android mobile phone

  • Download the stokisha App on your mobile phone
  • Register your business details
  • Proceed to apply a loan and provide access to your financial date.
  • Once an ammount is approved, you will receive a message from Stokisha
  • Then make your stock order.

You can also access the Stokisha on USSD by dialing *483*67# and follow the same process above.

How much can I borrow?

A minimum of KES 5,000 to am maximum of KES 300,000.

What profit rate does Stokisha charge?

0.5% per day with a 2.5% processing fees per loan application.

How do I repay my loan?

You will repay your loan using the Mpesa Paybill Number 597586 with your phone number as the account number.

What will happen if I delay my paymnet?

It will lead to default charges and jeopardize your existing credit limit and score.

What will happen if I do not repay my loan?

It will lead to tough credit collection measures, negative listing on CRB, and a drop in your credit rating.

Can I request to pay my loan at a later date?

Yes. A fee will be charged on the rollover of the principal amaount only.

What are the advantages of paying my loan on time?

It increases your credit rating and enhances your chances of getting a larger loan amount.
